Boston Exhibit 2013

I was fortunate and privileged to be able to organize a curated and juried exhibit in The McGladrey Art Gallery in Boston.

To date, the reception's attendance was the largest recorded- thanks to the hard work of many people combining business, art, and research. ArtBeyond helped to raise funds for much needed spinal cord injury research at MIT-Harvard labs. Please check out the gallery images below.
